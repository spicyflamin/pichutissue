Wojak : Describing the Ugly Truth of Society

exploring the Wojak meme's origins and its societal and existential implications.

Posted by Pichutissue on July 12, 2024

Wojak, is an Internet meme that is, in its original form, a simple, black-outlined cartoon drawing of a bald man with a wistful expression. The origin of the Wojak illustration is unknown. It may have emerged in 2009 on a Polish imageboard named vichan, from where it was later reposted to the German imageboard krautchan in 2010 by a poster called "wojak."

The meme subsequently grew in popularity on 4chan, where the character became associated with the phrases formerly used by wojak such as "I know that feel, bro", "that feel" or "that feel when". Countless variants of the meme have since emerged, expressing different emotions or representing various archetypes.

What was most significant about the lunar voyage was not that man set foot on the Moon but that they set eye on the earth.

There are 4 main variants of the Wojak, Boomer; coming from the word Baby Boomer. A person born during 1940-1960 is an ignorant old person who ignores what is going on in the world. Zoomer, is a person in Gen- Z from late 2000s to early 2010s who are teeangers-young adults who act cocky and that the world revolves around them. Bloomer, a person who realizes that society is ugly but chooses to see a bright light to things. A growth mindset. Finally a Doomer. Typically people born in the 1990s and early 2000s, typically people who grew up on the internet and finding out that the world is a horrible place and gave up on society. Secluding away from society.

These characters perfectly describe how much diversity there is in the world. Some are a blommer, some are zoomers, some may be boomers and some can be doomers.
